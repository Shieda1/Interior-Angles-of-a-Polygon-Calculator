What is the interior angles of a polygon?
The interior angles of a polygon is the formation of angles within the polygon that is formed by every pair of adjacent sides.
How to calculate sum of interior angles?
To calculate sum of interior angles you should use the following formula:
sum of interior angles = 180 * (number of sides of a polygon - 2)
